Step 1: ABC's of Character Creation

The ABC's of character creation involve:

  • A: Choosing an Ancestry that defines your character's race and unique traits.
  • B: Picking two Backgrounds that reflect your character's history and skills.
  • C: Creating three Connections that tie your character to people, places, or events.

Example: Kelsey the Medic chose the Human ancestry, Medic and Healer backgrounds, and has connections to her hometown hospital, a villain named Dr. Chaos, and a party member named Alex.

Step 2: Core Ability Assignment

Assign the ability score array [3, 3, 2, 2, 1, 1] to the six core stats:

  • Strength: Physical power
  • Dexterity: Agility and reflexes
  • Constitution: Endurance and resilience
  • Intelligence: Knowledge and reasoning
  • Wisdom: Perception and insight
  • Charisma: Influence and charm

Example: Kelsey distributed her scores as follows: Strength: 1, Dexterity: 2, Constitution: 3, Intelligence: 3, Wisdom: 2, Charisma: 1.
