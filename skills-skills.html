Skills

Below is a list of skills available for your characters. Each skills includes a name, the skill point Attribute, any types, and a description.

Skills are the driving force of adventuring and they all will relate back to a core attribute stat. You natural abilities will allow your nature skills to grow even further.

Skill types are a quick way to determine what they are primarily needed for - Combat skills are used in Combat and Adventuring skills are used while exploring.

Some Skills are setting specific, but they all will be listed here for a quick description and use of what they do.
