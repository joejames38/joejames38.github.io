Step 1: ABC's of Character Creation

The ABC's of character creation involve:

  • A: Choosing an Ancestry that defines your character's race and unique traits.
  • B: Picking two Backgrounds that reflect your character's history and skills.
  • C: Creating three Connections that tie your character to people, places, or events. Associate 1 skill that you would have gained with that connection, and take note to increase that skill later.

Example: Kelsey the Medic chose the Human ancestry, Healer and Mountainous backgrounds, and has connections to her hometown hospital (+1 Medicine), a villain named Dr. Chaos(+1 Insight), and a her gun enthusiast party member named Alex (+1 Ranged Weapons).

Step 2: Core Ability Assignment

Assign the ability score array [3, 3, 2, 2, 1, 1] to the six core stats:

  • Strength: Physical power
  • Dexterity: Agility and reflexes
  • Constitution: Endurance and resilience
  • Intelligence: Knowledge and reasoning
  • Wisdom: Perception and insight
  • Charisma: Influence and charm

Example: Kelsey is looking to be a support party member and has distributed her scores as follows: Strength: 1, Dexterity: 2, Constitution: 1, Intelligence: 3, Wisdom: 3, Charisma: 2.

Step 3: Spend Skill Points(SP) on Skills and Feats

Assign the starting Skills Points 24 to the arrays of skills availiable.

Choose a variety of skills since you never know what the story may need to solve a mystery.

Save a few Skill Points to spend on buying feats. These are much more expensive, but can be worth it for the added benefits.

Remember you can only add skill points to skills up to the max of their associated core ability score.

Example: Kelsey distributed her skill points as follows:
Strength: 1, Dexterity: 2, Constitution: 3, Intelligence: 3, Wisdom: 2, Charisma: 1.

  • +2 to Iniative
  • +1 to Ranged Weapons (Kelsey already has a +1 from her connection with Alex)
  • +1 to Fortide
  • +1 to Reflex (Only a small increase, but more can be spent here later)
  • +3 to Will
  • +2 to Medicine (+1 from working in a hospital to a max of +3)
  • +3 to Investigation
  • +1 to Science (Leaving room for improvement later)

Example: Kelsey now wants to buy a few feats.
Strength: 1, Dexterity: 2, Constitution: 3, Intelligence: 3, Wisdom: 2, Charisma: 1.

  • Tough 1 (Cost 5SP): To gain 5 more HP and meeting the prequisite of 1+ Constitution
  • Medic's Touch (Cost 5SP): To gain extra healing if her party needs it.

Kelsey has now finished allocating her initial skill points. There will be plenty more to come later.

Step 4: Equipment

Choose 1 set of Armor, Choose 1 weapon, and Choose 3 pieces of adventuring gear. Worn items don't count against your inventory limit of 10 + your Strength Score.

Some things are free to carry like a peronal items such as a wallet.

Example: Kelsey is looking to be a ranged fighter in combat:
Strength: 1, Dexterity: 2, Constitution: 1, Intelligence: 3, Wisdom: 3, Charisma: 2.

  • Light Armor DR+2
  • Sidearm
  • Spare Clip
  • Flashlight
  • Med Kit

Note: Kelsey has an inventory limit of 11 items - 10 + 1 Strenght .
