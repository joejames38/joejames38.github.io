About Character Hub

Character Hub is your all-in-one tool for creating, managing, and enhancing characters for tabletop role-playing games. Whether you're diving into an epic sci-fi adventure or exploring a fantastical medieval realm, Character Hub is designed to provide you with all the tools you need to bring your characters to life.

This project was built with tabletop enthusiasts in mind, offering features like character creation, skill management, feat selection, and even custom generators for different genres. The goal is to streamline the character creation process and make it more engaging and intuitive for players and game masters alike.

Explore, create, and let your imagination soar with Character Hub!
